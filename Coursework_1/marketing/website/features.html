What We Do


Sleep Tracking

Using an accelerometer and microphone to track movement and ambient noise levels, we feed the data into our algorithm to determine the sleep stage of the user.

Sleep Rating

Using our custom algorithms, the user's sleep is compared against an ideal nights sleep, in order to determine how well they slept.

Sleep Advice

Sleep Pillow learns with you. Depending on the quality of your sleep, the platform will attempt to give advice on how to optimize your sleep.

Power saving

When not in use, the device will enter power saving modes on the accelerometer and on the board, in order to reduce the power consumption of the device.

Data Visualization

The user is able to view the sleep stages they were in throughout the night, in order to see how they slept.

Power Saving

The client only sends data when it needs to! We process the data into the client side to minimise server processing load, only sending the sleep stage every ten minutes. This allows us to easily scale up the number of devices.
